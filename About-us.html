About us

Princess Nakedi Nonceba

CEO / Top chef

African Delites Catering & Services is a 100% Female black owned company born in the modern South Africa situated right in the heart of Germiston at 214 Meyer Street. We’re close to the OR Tambo Airport. African Delites, a place where culture would be shared and experienced. We specialise in African cuisine dishes, prepared with love. And our constant ability to provide fresh and tasty quality service will no doubt be able to sustain African Delites’ strong growth in its chosen area of competence through its unique style and commitment to service. African Delites Catering & Services gave birth to African Delites Restaurant which was formed purely for show casing African style cooking with the hope of attracting tourist to our Ekurhuleni, who would like to experience the taste of Africa and have a feel of our African style cooking. African Delites also gave birth African Delites Services which supplies corporate wear and corporate gifts. This was purely because we wanted to be a one stop shop for our clients.

Infrastructure

African Delites employs 10 staff members 4 Females and 6 Males all living in ekurhuleni who have been trained, and given experience in their relevant respective fields. With skilled staff we are able to offer comprehensive and efficient service nationally while following a proactive management philosophy that allows for development of personnel within the organisation, the expansion of individual competencies and capabilities, which in turn strengthens our intellectual strength.

Our Policy

African Delites’ policy is to supply high quality service while maintaining extremely competitive prices and our motor is “Do it right the first time”. It has been because of this policy that a large number of clients have turned to us for all their needs.
